(G)I-dle (Korean: (여자)아이들; RR: Yeoja Aideul), stylized in all caps, is a South Korean girl group formed by Cube Entertainment in 2018.
(G)I-dle debuted on May 2, 2018, with the single "Latata" from their first extended play (EP) I Am.
The group consists of five members: Miyeon, Minnie, Soyeon, Yuqi and Shuhua. Soojin left the lineup on August 14, 2021.
